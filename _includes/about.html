About

Art Frick is a web designer, developer, and online accessibility specialist in the greater NYC area. He is a full-time staff member of the University of New Hampshire's Insititute on Disability, where he does a lot of Drupal development, assists in developing technical specifications for several grants and programs at the Institute, and speaks about online accessibility and Drupal.

Art is always looking for new and exciting projects to be involved with.
