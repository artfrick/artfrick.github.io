About

Art strives to make the world a better, more inclusive, more accessible place through the use of information technology, project management, and innovative and compassionate leadership.

He has worked with world-class organizations, such as Wikipedia and the University of New Hampshire to deliver cutting-edge, inclusive, technological solutions as well as small businesses, startups, and individuals to realize and actualize goals and objectives.

Additionally, Art has presented on inclusive technology and web development at several regional and national conferences in recent years and has several presentations and posters to his credit.

Art pursues professional learning opportunities regularly to stay abreast of the most cutting-edge solutions available. He has a bachelor's degree in Fine Art from Parsons School of Design and is currently pursuing an MBA.
