About

Art Frick is a web designer, developer, and online accessibility specialist in the greater NYC area. He is a full-time staff member of the University of New Hampshire's Insititute on Disability, where he does a lot of Drupal development, assists in developing technical specifications for several grants and programs at the Institute, and is a frequent speaker about online accessibility issues and Drupal.

 

Art is always looking for new and exciting projects to be involved with! Art's worked extensively with many different web technologies, has presented on online accessibility issues to national audiences, and has spent many years transitioning ideas to reality. He has a fine arts degree from Parsons School of Design, plays lots of rock and roll, and is currently persuing an MBA at the University of New Hampshire.

 

He's currently updating the portfolio section of this website, especially after having been to the NJ Drupal Camp! Please send a message to check our his current work
